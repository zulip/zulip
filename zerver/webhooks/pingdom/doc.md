# Zulip Pingdom integration

Zulip supports integration with Pingdom and can notify you of
uptime status changes from your Pingdom dashboard.

{start_tabs}

1. {!create-channel.md!}

1. {!create-an-incoming-webhook.md!}

1. {!generate-webhook-url-basic.md!}

1. From the main menu, open the **Integrations** menu, and click on
    **Add integration**.

1. Select "Webhook" for **Type** and enter a name for the webhook.
    Paste the URL generated above in the webhook URL field and make
    sure the **Active** checkbox is checked. Click **Save integration**.

1. Ensure your integration is selected in the **Check** settings.

{end_tabs}
