# Link to a profile

Zulip allows you to easily copy a direct link to someone's profile.

## Link to a profile

{start_tabs}

{tab|desktop-web}

{!right-sidebar-view-profile.md!}

1. At the top of the user profile, click on the **link** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-link"></i>)
   icon located to the right of their name to copy a link to their profile.

!!! Tip ""

    You can also click on a user's profile picture or name on a
    message they sent to open their **user card**.

{end_tabs}
