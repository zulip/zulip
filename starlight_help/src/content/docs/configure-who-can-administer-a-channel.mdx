## Configure who can administer a channel

<FlattenedSteps>
  <NavigationSteps target="relative/channel/all" />

  1. Select a channel.

  <SelectChannelViewPermissions />

  1. Under **Administrative permissions**, configure **Who can administer
     this channel**.

  <SaveChanges />
</FlattenedSteps>
