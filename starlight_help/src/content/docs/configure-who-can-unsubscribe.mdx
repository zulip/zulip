You can configure who can unsubscribe themselves from channels. There is an
[organization-wide
permission](#configure-who-can-unsubscribe-from-channels-in-general), which by
default lets everyone unsubscribe themselves from any channel. You can also
configure who can unsubscribe themselves from a [specific
channel](#configure-who-can-unsubscribe-from-a-specific-channel).

Organization administrators, [channel
administrators](/help/configure-who-can-administer-a-channel), and users who can
[unsubscribe anyone](/help/configure-who-can-unsubscribe-others) can always
unsubscribe themselves.

## Configure who can unsubscribe from channels in general

<AdminOnly />

<FlattenedSteps>
  <NavigationSteps target="settings/organization-permissions" />

  1. Under **Channel permissions**, configure **Who can unsubscribe from
     channels**.

  <SaveChanges />
</FlattenedSteps>

## Configure who can unsubscribe from a specific channel

If you have permission to administer a channel, you can configure who can
unsubscribe themselves from it.

<FlattenedSteps>
  <NavigationSteps target="relative/channel/all" />

  1. Select a channel.

  <SelectChannelViewPermissions />

  1. Under **Subscription permissions**, configure **Who can unsubscribe from
     this channel**.

  <SaveChanges />
</FlattenedSteps>
