This page documents features specific to Miatsuco's fork of Zulip. They are
not part of upstream Zulip, and won't appear in Zulip's own documentation.

## Configure whether previews of uploaded images and videos are shown

<AdminOnly />

You can configure whether uploaded images and videos are shown as inline
thumbnail previews in the message feed, or as plain links to the file. This
does not affect uploaded audio files, which are always shown as a playable
inline preview.

See also: [Image, video and website
previews](/help/image-video-and-website-previews) for the related, upstream
Zulip settings controlling previews of linked (non-uploaded) images, videos,
and websites.

<ZulipNote>
  Thumbnails are generated for uploaded images and videos regardless of this
  setting. However, Zulip renders each message's content once and caches it;
  changing this setting does not retroactively update how already-sent
  messages are displayed. It affects new messages going forward, and any
  existing message that is later edited.
</ZulipNote>

<FlattenedSteps>
  <NavigationSteps target="settings/organization-settings" />

  1. Under **Message feed settings**, toggle **Show thumbnail previews of
     uploaded images and videos**.

</FlattenedSteps>
