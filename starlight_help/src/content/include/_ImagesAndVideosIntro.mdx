Zulip shows previews of attached images, videos, and audio files, and lets you
click on a preview to [view an image in more
detail](/help/view-images-and-videos#use-the-image-viewer) or [play a
video](/help/view-images-and-videos#use-the-video-player). You can
[browse](/help/view-images-and-videos#browse-images-and-videos) all images and
videos attached to messages in your current view in the image viewer.
